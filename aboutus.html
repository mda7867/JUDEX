Our Mission



Due to unfairness within the Justice system, there is an imbalance regarding the incarceration and sentencing standards of the United States Justice System. As a
result of this imbalance, people of color such as African Americans are more likely to face negative judicial bias, which results in harsher punishments compared
to other races. To reduce the unfair bias in the system by identifying bad actors, Judex aims to enable a more balanced judicial system.
